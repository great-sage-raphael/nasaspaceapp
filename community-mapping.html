Community Mapping

Community mapping is the process of creating a visual representation (usually a map) of a community’s physical and social resources, challenges, and opportunities. It involves gathering data about a specific area (like a neighborhood, town, or region) and displaying it spatially to better understand how different factors (geography, infrastructure, demographics, and environment) interact within that space.
